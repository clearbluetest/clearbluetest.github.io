2 KEY HORMONES activated prior to ovulation: ESTROGEN: produced by ovaries Luteinising Hormone (LH): triggered by estrogen In response to the increase in estrogen levels, the hypothalamic-pituitary complex
STARTS TO PRODUCE LH 24-36HRS after the LH surge has happened, ovulation occurs After ovulation the egg survives for ~ 12-24HRS
Sperm can survive for up to 5 DAYS THE FULL FERTILITY WINDOW IS TYPICALLY 6 DAYS Identifying additional days of high fertility BEFORE OVULATION is important in
improving the chances of conception
